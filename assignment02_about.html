About

The Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Science, Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) is proud to organise the 2nd International Conference on Mathematical Sciences and Statistics (ICMSS2016). This international conference aims to bring together academicians, researchers and scientists for knowledge sharing in Mathematics and Statistics areas. The ICMSS2016 serves as good platforms for the scientific community members to meet with each other and to exchange ideas.

Objectives

  1. To present the most recent discoveries in Mathematics and Statistics.
  2. To serve as a platform for knowledge and information interchange between the experts from industrial and academic sectors.
  3. To identify potential collaboration among the participants.
