Agenda

TOPICS: The interest areas of the conference include but are not limited to:

  1. Applied Mathematics & Modeling

    • Numerical Analysis
    • Mathematical Computational Techniques
    • Fluid Mechanics
    • Heat and Mass Transfer
    • Differential Equations (ODEs and PDEs)
    • Mathematical Physics
    • Dynamical System & Chaos
    • Educational Mathematics
    • Operations Research
    • Optimization

  2. Statistics & Its Applications

    • Stochastic Systems
    • Statistics
    • Linear and Nonlinear Programming
    • Probability
    • Financial Mathematics
    • Acturial Mathematics

  3. Analysis & Its Applications

    • Functional Analysis
    • Set Theory
    • Matrix Theory
    • Approximation Theory
    • Calculus Variation

  4. Algebra & Its Applications

    • Algebra & Mathematical Logic
    • Number Theory
    • Applied Algebra
    • Cryptography
    • Groups, Rings & Modules
    • Topology
    • Algebraic Geometry

  5. Geometry & Its Applications

    • Geometry
    • Game Theory
    • Differential Geometry
    • Fractals
